# TurtleBox v0.3 Candidate Features

> List only. No design, no development, no sprint planning.

| # | Feature | User Value | Dev Cost | Priority |
|---|---------|------------|----------|----------|
| 1 | Multiple save slots (4 slots) | Save at different game points, boss fights, levels | Low (~160 lines) | High |
| 2 | Save state preview | See what was happening when saved (thumbnail) | Medium (~100 lines) | Medium |
| 3 | Recent ROMs list | Quick access to recently played games | Low (~50 lines) | Medium |
| 4 | Quick reset (Ctrl+R) | Restart ROM without relaunching | Low (~10 lines) | Medium |
| 5 | Window size memory | Remember window size/position across sessions | Low (~30 lines) | Low |
| 6 | Second controller support | P2 also on gamepad | Medium (~80 lines) | Medium |
| 7 | Rewind (hold R) | Reverse emulation frames | High (~300 lines, performance) | Low |
| 8 | Screenshot (F12) | Save current frame as PNG | Low (~30 lines) | Low |
| 9 | Cheat codes (Game Genie) | Enter cheat codes for NES games | Medium (~100 lines) | Low |
| 10 | Shader / CRT filter | Visual retro effect | High (~200+ lines, OpenGL) | Low |
